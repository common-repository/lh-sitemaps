Sick of overly complex sitemap plugins and want something that just works? Or are you a developer and want full flexibility over your sitemap content? Well this plugin is for you,

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my xml sitemaps? =

Two xml sitemaps are created. A general xml sitemap available at: /sitemap-general.xml and a google news sitemap available at /sitemap-news.xml.

= How do I create a html sitemap? =

Place the short code [lh_html_sitemap] anywhere in the your post_content on a page of custom post type. The shortcode has no parameters, it just works.

== Installation ==

1. Upload folder to '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Re generate permalinks by going to Settings->Permalinks
